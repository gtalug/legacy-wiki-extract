GTALUG at Linux World Canada 2006

The Greater Toronto Area Linux User Group will be doing things with and at the 2006 Linux World Canada trade show. Currently in planning are the following:

  • A booth on the show floor.
  • The NewTLUG meeting to be held at the show

GTALug members can get free trade show admission, and a 25% discount off the advanced and regular price conference fees, details of the discount can be seen below.

The show runs April 24th - 26th, 2006 at the Metro Toronto Convention Centre, North Building.

General information about the show can be found here.

A look at the last show can be seen here.
