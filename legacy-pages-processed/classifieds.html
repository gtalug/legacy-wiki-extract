This is a person who is passionate about the big picture since every decision is made by taking into account what would be best for the whole system. The Integration Engineer is someone who takes a holistic approach to technology and is excited about tackling issues that pose a multi-layered challenge.

Being an Integration Engineer means that you are involved early in the design process to give a holistic perspective. You are the gatekeeper of the entire development process. You look for ways that the infrastructure might fail, as well as ways to optimize the system. You will do light feature testing to ensure that new/upgraded code doesn’t interrupt existing resources. Essentially you are a key member of a team that ensures OANDA’s continued success.
