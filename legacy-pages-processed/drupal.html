Drupal

Wikipedia:Drupal is an open source "content management" platform for managing a variety of sorts of web sites. It runs in PHP atop Apache or IIS, pretty well any OS (but notably Linux!), a DBMS (e.g. - PostgreSQL or MySQL; other DBMSes are supported.

It is highly customizable, but includes the following sorts of things:

  • Polls
  • Templating
  • Threaded comments
  • Blogging
  • Content syndication (RSS)
  • Permalinks
  • Discussion forums
