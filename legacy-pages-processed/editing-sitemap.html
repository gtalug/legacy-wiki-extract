From GTALUG

When you go to add your link into any of the sitemap pages, you will find that there are a lot of {{Blah}} type tags. These are templates for outline style organization sections that get used on multiple pages. This is good, because you only need to decide what section your list goes under, and then add your link in the template and it will be correct for all sitemap subpages, etc.

There are links uncer the edit box to the templates used that will take you directly there. The other option is to manually go there. For example, to edit {{Blah}} you would go to [[Template:Blah]]
