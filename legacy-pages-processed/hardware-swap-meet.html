Getting together to swap unused hardware.
[edit]

Proposal

I am hoping to organise a general meeting of GTALUG members and/or attendees for the purpose of swapping the unused hardware that some of us have lying around to build as many working computers as possible. If you happen to have working computers that are too slow for your purposes and/or are just taking up space that you need, these would be welcome too.

Please edit this page to indicate what hardware you might be willing to part with and also add items that you would like into the appropriate section. (i.e Hardware Available or Hardware Wish List)

Please put your name or E-Mail address on the line beneath you edit of the lists, so that things stay somewhat organised.

Amos H. Weatherill
