About Meetings at IBM 3600 Steeles

**Security Requirements** Due to security requirements at IBM, all attendees are required to have a security badge. Badges are prepared in advance.

If you plan to attend, please send your name to Paul Mora. Please remember to do so no later than the day before the meeting.

Please be sure to return badges to the front reception at the end of the session.

Location: IBM offices 3600 Steeles Ave East, north side of Steeles at Pharmacy/Esna Park (between Victoria Park and Warden) | Map of Location

Directions:

Meet at the front entrance well before 7:00pm (6:30 recommended) to pick up your ID badge. At about 7:00 attendees will be escorted to the auditorium. Some provision will be made for people that arrive a little late.

Parking: Parking is available in the visitor parkade from 6:00pm to 11:00pm.
