IT360 Conference

In previous years, this was called RealWorldLinux. Since 2007, it has been called IT360, and covers some additional topics over previous years...

  • For 2003 and 2004, it was called Real World Linux.
  • For 2005 and 2006, it was called LinuxWorld Canada.
  • In 2007, the dates were 2007-05-01 and 2007-05-02.
  • In 2008, the conference and expo is scheduled for April 7-9, 2008.
