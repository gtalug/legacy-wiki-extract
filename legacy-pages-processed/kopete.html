Kopete

Kopete is the KDE Instant Messanger client. Supporting a plug-in architecture, it supports many of the popular instant messaging protocols including AIM, ICQ, MSN, Yahoo, Jabber, IRC, Gadu-Gadu, Novell GroupWise Messenger, and more.
