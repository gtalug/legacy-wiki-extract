New Mailing Lists

Lists will be migrated over to the new Mailman interface as we work out the bugs with it/people volunteer to help with this task.

[edit]

hw@gtalug.org

This is a mailing list for hardware specific issues, discussion, etc. The idea is to move the generic hardware discussions that have been happening on the main list that do not necessarily have a lot to do with Linux to a more specific list. To subscribe go to:

http://lists.gtalug.org/mailman/listinfo/hw

[edit]

ms@gtalug.org

This is a mailing list for Microsoft related discussion that is not technically related to Linux integration, etc, but may be interesting to some list members.

http://lists.gtalug.org/mailman/listinfo/ms

GTALUG Mailing List

This is a general purpose list -- for Linux announcements, technical questions, and other information. Subscribing to this list will automatically subscribe you to the GTALUG-Announce list.

This list is for the discussion of Linux related issues, news, problems, and ideas. This list is not anyone's personal soapbox. It is not for the discussion of political, social, or religious issues, bad jokes, personal rants or similar non Linux related discussion. Unsolicited commercial advertising will not be tolerated. Any subscribers who offers or requests pirated software, license keys or cracks, and similar will be dropped from the list without warning.

Off topic messages will not be tolerated or endorsed. First time offenders will be warned. Repeat offenders will be restricted from posting to the list for a 1 month period, or until such time that the list managers feel the offender is capable of following the rules.

There is also a The GTALUG Mailing List archive.

Please send a mail to:
tlug-request@tlug.ss.org
And in the body of the message, place only:
"subscribe" to subscribe or "unsubscribe" to unsubscribe, without the quotes.
[edit]

GTALUG-Announce Mailing List

This is strictly an announcement list, not an interactive forum, from which you will receive important notices from the GTALUG administrators. Subscribing to this list will not automatically subscribe you to the GTALUG forum list.

Please send a mail to:
tlug-announce-request@tlug.ss.org
And in the body of the message, place only:
"subscribe" to subscribe or "unsubscribe" to unsubscribe, without the quotes.
[edit]

MythGTA Mailing List

There is a greater Toronto, ON area MythTV oriented mailing list.

Please send an e-mail to:
mythtv-request@ss.org
Leave the subject line blank.
And in the body of the message, place only:
"subscribe" to subscribe or "unsubscribe" to unsubscribe, without the quotes.

You will get back an e-mail that offers additional information.
