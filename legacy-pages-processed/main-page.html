From GTALUG

Hello and welcome to Greater Toronto Area Linux User Group or GTALUG (formerly known as TLUG). GTALUG is committed to the free exchange of Linux-related information. Our goals as a user group include the creation of mechanisms that allow people with a common interest in linux to socialize. See about and our manifesto for more information.
