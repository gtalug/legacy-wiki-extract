Dear Linuxers,

The next meeting of the Toronto Linux Users Group will be held
at 8 pm in the North York Public Library on Monday, January
30.

The subject will be setting up UUCP  under Linux,
and the speakers will be Adrian Buss and Darren Inksetter.

Adrian and Darren will cover some or all of the
following topics:

-brief description of hdb vs taylor mode
-some examples of taylor config files
-uucp via tcp/ip
-basic smail set up to work with innuendo as a smart host or any other
 site for that matter
-some basic inter relations between news, (inn and cnews) smail and uucp
 permissons

The talk will be followed by a question period and informal discussion.

        Location:       Meeting Room 2
                        North York Public Library
                        5120 Yonge Street

        How to Get There:

                       By the subway: take the Yonge subway
                       northbound to the "North York City
                       Centre" stop. Once there, follow
                       the signs to the "City Hall", which
                       is the same building as the library.

                       If driving: ample parking is available
                       one block west of the library,
                       on Beecroft Street.

Everyone is welcome to attend.  Be there!

  Cheers,

        Laszlo Herczeg
