Dear Linuxers,

The next meeting of the Toronto Linux Users Group will be held
at 7:30 pm at the Swan Building, 5415 Dundas Street West,
on December 20, 1995.

The featured of the speaker of the meeting will be Greg Weir,
Senior Web Developer at Internet Direct. The topic of his
presentation will be the Java programming language.

Special thanks go to Internet Direct for donating their state-of-the-art
multimedia conference room for this and subsequent Linux meetings.

Location:   Suite 301
            5415 Dundas Street West (just west of Kipling)
            Etobicoke          

How to Get There:

           By the subway: take the Bloor subway
           to the Kipling station.  The Swan building is
           two blocks west of the Kipling station,
           next to the large "Canadian Tire" plaza.

           For those who are driving, ample and free parking
           is available in the parking lot just south
           of the Swan building.

Everyone is welcome to attend. Be there!
