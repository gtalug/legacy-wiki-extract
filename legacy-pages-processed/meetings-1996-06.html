Dear Linuxers,

The next meeting of the Toronto Linux Users Group will be held
at 7:30 pm at the Swan Building, 5415 Dundas Street West,
on Tuesday, June 25, 1996.

The speaker will be Derek B. Snider, senior programmer
at Internet Direct Canada.

This meeting will discuss the steps necessary for
upgrading your old Linux installation to the newly
released 2.0 kernel series.

The new kernel offers many new features and performance
increases over  the 1.2 and 1.3 series, but it requires
significant modifications to an existing Linux installation.

Derek will address the issue of how to upgrade safely and
efficiently an existing production-grade Linux system.
