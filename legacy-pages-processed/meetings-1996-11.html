Dear Unix Users,

The November meeting of the Toronto Linux Users Group will be held
at 7:30 pm at the Swan Building, 5415 Dundas Street West,
on Tuesday, November 26, 1996.

The topic of this meeting will be "New Realeases from Caldera"

The meeting will provide hands-on experience with Caldera's
commercial grade software for the Linux platform.
The software  presented will include:

-WABI(tm) -- Sun Microsystems Windows Emulator
-WordPerfect(tm) - Corel's WordPerfect, with full graphical capabilities
-Caldera Open Linux - Caldera's latest installation system

The presenter will be Evan Leibovitch of Sound Software Inc. Evan is
a well known  figure in Toronto Unix circles, with extensive background
in commercial Unix systems.

There will be two special door prizes at this meeting, courtesy of Yggdrasil
Computing.

I'd like to take this oppurtunity also to cordially invite you to
visit our new home page which is located at:

   <URL:http://abyss.idirect.com:/tlug/>

Produced and maintained by Michel Afar, this page provides an
excellent starting point for exploring Linux resources on the
Internet and contains pointers to our meetings and other
activities in the Toronto area.

Meeting details follow:

Location:   Suite 207
            5415 Dundas Street West
            Etobicoke          

How to Get There:

           By the subway: take the Bloor subway
           to the Kipling station.  The Swan building is
           two blocks west of the Kipling station,
           next to the large "Canadian Tire" plaza.

           For those who are driving, ample and free parking
           is available in the parking lot just south
           of the Swan building.

Everyone is welcome to attend.
