SIMPL Messaging.

Presented by John Collins and Bob Findlay.

TLUG meetings are held at UofT on the second Tuesday of each month at 7:30 pm.

Date
Tuesday November 9th, 1999
Time
7:30 pm


Topic
SIMPL Messaging
Speakers
John Collins and Bob Findlay
FCsoftware was formed in 1991 as a partnership between the two principals, John Collins and Robert Findlay. Between the two founding partners there is over 40 man years of software development expertise, including over 20 man years under the QNX operating system (realtime POSIX compliant OS) and 2 man years on the open LINUX (including RTLinux) OS's. In addition to the expertise of the principals we have a growing network of virtual partners who can be teamed on any given project.
Description
Send/Receive/Reply messaging first pioneered by commercial OS's such as QNX, has proved to be both an elegant way to write code as well as an elegant way to design code. The SIMPL open source project is dedicated to bringing this functionality to Linux. (http://www.holoweb.net/~simpl)
FCsoftware Inc. is acting as the facilitator for the SIMPL project.
