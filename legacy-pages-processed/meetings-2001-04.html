Sun Cobalt's Sausalito architecture.

Presented by Mike Waychison.

TLUG meetings are held at UofT on the second Tuesday of each month at 7:30 pm.


Date
Tuesday April 10th, 2001
Time
7:30 pm (Q & A at 7pm)


Topic
Sun Cobalt's Sausalito architecture.
Speaker
Mike Waychison
Description
Mike Waychison will be talking about Sun Cobalt's Sausalito architecture used in their Qube3 and upcoming products. Docs and the SDK for the Sausalito Architecture for Sun Cobalt Hardware is located at http://developer.cobalt.com The site requires a free registration though to access the docs. The SDK can also be found on the ftp server... ftp://ftp.cobaltnet.com/pub/developer/TechNotes/SSDK.pdf
