Speaker
Christopher Browne
Born at an early age, to a couple that met at the computer department at Revenue Canada in the early '60s, Chris rather honestly inherited an understanding of both accounting and computing. Throwing in a B.Math in accounting and computer science, and an M.Sc in Systems Science, Chris has been known to pretend to know something about math, computer science, and occasionally accounting. Having some years of experience doing systems integration and programming with the SAP R/3 ERP system, he now works as a database administrator, working with PostgreSQL, with the .ORG and .INFO
Description
GnuCash. It's tax season, and it is doubtless too late to start setting up a proper set of books for the tax man if you haven't already. GnuCash is an increasingly powerful package to help with personal finances, and might help you next year...
