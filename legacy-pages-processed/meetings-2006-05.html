Topic

WAJAX

Speaker

Scott Elcomb

Description

Scott will be discussing WAJAX. Scott will be providing an introduction to the topic, discussing the conceptual basis and providing a walk though. He will then follow-up with a WAJAX development bootcamp including an implementation of "Hello World" and discussing the possibility of a better browser. Scott will then be tying up some loose ends, discussing milestones for the project and concluding with time for Q & A.
