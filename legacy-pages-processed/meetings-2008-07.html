Topic

New chip advances from Sun

Speakers

Russell Crook

Description

In the last few years, Sun has been developing multi-threaded chips with more than just two threads, primarily to deal with the mismatch between fast CPUs and slow memory. The newest chips are open-sourced, and we can now talk about them.

  • OpenSPARC.net
  • Sun OpenSPARC
  • UltraSPARC T1 - the product that has been "open sourced"
  • Rock Processor - the alternate successor to UltraSPARC T1/T2
