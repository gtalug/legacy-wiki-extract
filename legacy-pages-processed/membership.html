 Becoming a member.

The membership is generally informal and consists of people who have an interest in Linux and wish to exchange information and ideas with other like minded individuals.

We all have one thing in common: a desire to network with others to help us understand, encourage us to explore the varied aspects, and to gain exposure to diverse and emerging Linux technologies. See also the Manifesto.

GTALUG is made up of many different people, from programmers and technicians who make their living as information technology professionals, to people who have no computer experience whatsoever. We welcome all to share and explore with us the freedom, power and fun Linux has to offer as an operating system for people of all interests and needs.

There are four notable vehicles for further participation:

  • Signing up for Mailing_lists
  • Coming to meetings of either TLUG or NewTLUG
  • Volunteering to do things
  • Formal GTALUG membership
