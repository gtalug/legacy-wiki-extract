CLC Newbie LUG

Are you a newcomer to Linux? Then we invite you to attend the Newbie LUG that is starting up this month at the CLUE Linux Centre in Toronto. The first meeting will be held Tuesday, October 26 at 7 PM.

The topics of this meeting will include "How to choose / how to get Linux," as well as an introduction to the new users' group and planning for future meetings. It is anticipated that the Newbie LUG will meet regularly on the fourth Tuesday of every month.

The CLUE Linux Centre is located at 169 Eastern Avenue. For directions, visit http://centre.linux.ca/directions.html. If you are planning to attend please RSVP to Evan Leibovitch at evan@starnix.com.
