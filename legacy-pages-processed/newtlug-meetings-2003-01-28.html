From GTALUG

This month's NewTLUG meeting will be held Tues Jan 28th., at the IBM offices 3600 Steeles Ave E.

Linux, your digital camera, and you!

Join Linux author, columnist, and guru, Marcel Gagné, as he shows you the tricks of the trade when it comes to working with Linux and your digital camera. It's easier than you think . . . As a special added bonus, Marcel will report back on the 2003 LinuxWorld Expo in New York city (having taken place just days before). He'll tell you what he saw, what he heard, what was rumoured, what's coming, and what isn't in the world of Linux.

For lots of Linux goodies, make sure you visit Marcel's website at http://www.marcelgagne.com .
