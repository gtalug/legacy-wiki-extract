This month's NewTLUG meeting will be held Tues Jan 27th., at the IBM offices 3600 Steeles Ave E.


Thanks: to Peter for helping NewTLUG with room arrangements and looking after the badges.

Badges: email PWArmstrong to pre-register

Date: Tues May 25th.

Time: 7-10pm

Topic:

Evan Leibovitch and a colleague of his at LPI (Linux Professional Institute) will give an overview of the state of Linux on the world scene based on their experience and attendance at conferences around the world, most recently in Geneva, Capetown and New York. Evan will also comment on Linux in the news/media. There will be time for audience discussion and comment.

Location: IBM offices 3600 Steeles Ave East, north side of Steeles at Pharmacy/Esna Park (between Victoria Park and Warden)

Directions: Meet at the front entrance well before 7:00pm (6:30 recommended) to pickup your ID badge. At about 7:00 we'll be escorted to the auditorium. Some provision will be made for anyone arriving a little late.

Parking: Parking is available in the visitor parkade from 6:00pm to 11:00pm.
