NewTlug Meetings:2007-10-23

This month's NewTLUG meeting will be held Tues October 23rd., at the IBM offices 3600 Steeles Ave E.

-- Important -- all attendees will be required to have a security badge.

Badges should be prepared in advance. If you plan to attend, please send your name to Paul (off-list at <pmora@ca.ibm.com>) preferably, before Monday October 21st. ...and please be sure to return badges to the front reception at the end of the session.

Date: October 23, 2007

Time: 7:00 to 10:00pm

Presenter: Colin McGregor

Topic:

This month's NewTLUG meeting will be in two parts.

For the first part of the meeting Colin McGregor will be looking at what has changed in the world MythTV. In particular the talk will look at HDTV, what are the issues? This part of the talk will pay particular attention to issues that apply to Roger's cable subscribers.

The second part of the meeting will be a question and answer session done along the lines of a Unix Unanimous meeting, This session will be moderated by Colin McGregor. So, bring your questions, and we will try and get you some answers.

Thanks: to Paul for helping NewTLUG not only by arranging for a room and hosting our meetings at IBM but also with looking after the badges.

Badges: please email Paul Mora <pmora@ca.ibm.com> to pre-register
