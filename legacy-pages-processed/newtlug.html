NewTLUG

New Users Special Interest Group of the Toronto Linux Users' Group
NewTLUG is an offshoot of the Toronto Linux Users' Group, designed to complement the existing monthly meetings aimed at experienced Linux Users.
NewTLUG aims to provide a welcoming atmosphere for people in the Greater Toronto Area who are new to Linux, looking to new things to do with their Linux systems, or are simply curious.
NewTLUG has its own web site. For more information, please visit newtlug.linux.ca
