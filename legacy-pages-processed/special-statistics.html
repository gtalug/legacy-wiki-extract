From GTALUG

GTALUG statistics

There are 1,801 total pages in the database. This includes "talk" pages, pages about GTALUG, minimal "stub" pages, redirects, and others that probably don't qualify as content pages. Excluding those, there are 519 pages that are probably legitimate content pages.

There have been a total of 2,186,354 page views, and 6,414 page edits since the wiki was setup. That comes to 3.56 average edits per page, and 340.87 views per edit.

User statistics

There are 4,401 registered users, of which 9 (or 0.20%) are administrators (see Project:Administrators).
