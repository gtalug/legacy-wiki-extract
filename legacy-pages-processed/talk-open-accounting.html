Open accounting has been a concept I've been kicking around for some time. I feel that it ought to be standardized at the government level. After all, I'm not particularly happy about my tax dollars being quietly shuffled around without my knowledge or approval. Well, at any rate.. I suppose a user group accounting project would be as good a start as any. -- Sy / (talk) 18:20, 15 May 2005 (EDT)

Organization and discussion would still work well on a wiki though. -- Sy / (talk) 11:29, 25 May 2005 (EDT)
