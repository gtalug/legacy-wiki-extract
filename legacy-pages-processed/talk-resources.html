cbbrowne At the 2006-01-22 board meeting, we discussed the notion of setting up a central "Resources" page whose URL we would put onto business cards for distribution at the IT360 conference.

The notion was that we should pick some set of topics that are likely to be of some general interest that we have some local expertise on. For each topic, we should have pointers both to traditional "global" resources (e.g. - to http://samba.org/, for SAMBA) as well as to local resources that might be able to render local assistance.
