- Systems / peripherals

  • Canada Computers -- Good prices, good selection.
  • Computer Warehouse Outlet Inc. -- Used equipment Sheppard/Allen.

  • TigerDirect.ca -- Physical stores in Markham and Etobicoke.

  • Active Surplus -- Good but strange shop for old hardware, Queen near University.
  • Sayel Electronics -- Several locations, head office Victoria Park & Steeles.
