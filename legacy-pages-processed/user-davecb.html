David Collier-Brown

I'm a Solaris and Linux performance engineer and capacity planner, formerly with Sun, and still mostly consulting for them through Trek Logic or Data Center Works.

In my Copious Spare Time I support Samba (and once wrote about it for O'Reilly) and do the occasional talk for TLUG and conferences.

I happily consult on

  • Performance Analysis and Capacity Planning, with Teamquest
  • Porting to and from almost every kind of Unix. If you are moving something to Linux, email me and see if I have spare time to donate.
  • Solaris customs and specials
  • Data Center management, maturity and stability
  • Remote systems management, and
  • Samba.
