> Headers -- I rather prefer having more whitespace between the horizontal rules and such because I find it to be more readable. I take it that you want to make the header smaller to give more room for content?

The problem is that if we have people with lower resolutions, even 1024x768 the actual useful content of the page ends up being somewhere where they can't see it (bottom of page)f. It's not a problem for higher resolutions, but it is fairly ugly to see nothing but page garbage at the top of the page. Maybe we should ask people what they prefer?

Another possibility would be to put things like the navigational links at the bottom of each page, but I find that kind of annoying too as then you've got to scroll down to see them. -- Leah / (talk)

Hmm.. I like the headers staying as headers.. I find it much easier to navigate through things without scrolling down. I don't mind the way things are, and if lower resolution users have a concern then that's good enough for me to remove whitespaces. Yes, perhaps we should ask around to see if there really is a concern, but to be honest it feels a bit more ethical to just consider them without making it a big deal. Actually, the one thing I'd like to see is the ability to turn the sidebar off with the default skin.. I hate wasting that space. -- Sy / (talk)
