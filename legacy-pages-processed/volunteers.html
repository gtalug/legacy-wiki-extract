Volunteering.

Any user group is only as good as its people. We have a lot of good folk at our meetings, and your volunteering shares the load and helps make the group better for everyone.

Currently the following tasks require help:

  • Co-ordinating the booking of speakers for meetings. Contact Herb Richter.
  • Co-ordinating TLUG/NEWTLUG staffing at booths for upcoming tradeshows.
  • Publicity (helping to get the word out as widely as possible)
