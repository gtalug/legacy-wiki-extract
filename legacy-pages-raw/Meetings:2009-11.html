Meetings:2009-11

From GTALUG


Tuesday November 10, 2009 at 7:30 pm

[edit]

Topic

BASH 4.0 and the book Pro Bash Programming

[edit]

Speakers

Chris F. A. Johnson

[edit]

Description

Chris F. A. Johnson will be talking about two things, the new features of Bash 4.0, and his new book, Pro Bash Programming.

[edit]

Location

TBA, but will likely be: 

   Room GB119, Galbraith Building, University of Toronto
   35 St George St
   Toronto, Ontario M5S 3G8 
   University of Toronto
[edit]

Schedule

6:00 PM - There will be an informal get together of Linux fans at the Pho Hung restaurant 350 Spadina Ave. to talk about Linux related topics over Vietnamese style food.

7:30 PM - Meeting and presentation.

Late evening - After the presentation a large group of Linux fans will move to a nearby pub for further informal socializing.
