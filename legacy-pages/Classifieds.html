   * Continually improve the performance and scalability of the site

System Integration Engineer for Downtown Toronto Company

Why OANDA?

OANDA is a unique company. It's not a Web upstart or a bank. It’s a technology company that innovates both in computer engineering and financial modeling for the Foreign Currency Exchange Trade market. OANDA has experienced continued growth since its founding in 1996, and opportunities for employees remain unprecedented as the company creates new products that redefine the forex market. With a daily capitalization of $3 trillion, the forex market dwarfs both the US bond and equity markets.

What's at the root of OANDA's success? The Employees. As an employee you will constantly develop innovative technology in an almost lab-like environment. You will develop and challenge your skills every day. You will have the ability to be self-directed in your work within a collaborative team structure. You’ll enjoy the immediacy of a small company, but use the most advanced tools/technologies that a large firm can offer. And to maintain that community feel at OANDA we offer flexibility & sociability with things like catered lunches, on-site massage therapists, Friday @ 4 gatherings and more. You will contribute to the growth and direction of OANDA, and your contributions to the success of the company will be recognized and rewarded.

Located in downtown Toronto, the OANDA development office provides convenient access to public transit and other downtown amenities. For more information about OANDA, visit our website at: www.oanda.com

The Integration Engineer:

At OANDA the Integration Engineer can be described as an Architect, System Administrator, Developer, and QA – all in one role. This is a person who is passionate about the big picture since every decision is made by taking into account what would be best for the whole system. The Integration Engineer is someone who takes a holistic approach to technology and is excited about tackling issues that pose a multi-layered challenge.

Being an Integration Engineer means that you are involved early in the design process to give a holistic perspective. You are the gatekeeper of the entire development process. You look for ways that the infrastructure might fail, as well as ways to optimize the system. You will do light feature testing to ensure that new/upgraded code doesn’t interrupt existing resources. You will write scripts to help automate things. You work closely with others and help guide them to the most appropriate solutions. Essentially you are a key member of a team that ensures OANDA’s continued success.

The Integration Engineer is also an excellent role to get a complete understanding of all the technology positions in both the Development and Operations parts of OANDA. This allows for a wide range of continued career growth opportunities within the company and beyond.

The Skills You Bring:

• A University degree in computer science, computer engineering, or a related field.
• Demonstrated knowledge, passion and experience (either professional or personal) of system architecture including communication protocols, intra-system dependencies, hardware and software configuration.
• Extensive experience with UNIX / Linux / Solaris operating systems.
• Preferred development experience using Perl and mod Perl (or other script languages).
• Sound knowledge of Object Oriented Programming concepts and experience with languages such as C++ and Java.
• Knowledge of build tools (make, Ant).
• Experience or knowledge of Database programming (particularly DB2).
• Ability to effectively communicate ideas and enjoy working with teams.
