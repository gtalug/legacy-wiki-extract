Summaries

The more familiar I get with these issues, the more I'd like to see them dealt with. It was the motivation behind getting Richard Stallman's talk today on video. If a message is to spread effectively, it needs to spread accross the widest range of media possible. -- dl33t::talk 06:53, 18 May 2005 (EDT)

  1. One good resource is Matthew Skala's Report from Ottawa; Top 12 copyright reform issues. The document is a couple years old but is, for the most part, still applicable.
  2. Another is this amazing response I got from the same thread that yielded the above link. It's going to take more than a few days to prepare my own summary, so I'm duplicating and linking to it here. Hopefully to start a mindshare.  :-)
