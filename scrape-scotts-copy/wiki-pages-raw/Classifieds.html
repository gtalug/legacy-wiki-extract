Seasoned Python Developer Wanted

(Posted 24 July 2011)

This is an opportunity to take on the challenging task of managing a sophisticated web application

About You
The successful candidate will be a senior programmer (or very skilled intermediate), fluent in our key programming languages (below), good with people and helpful to our clients.
He or she is a self-starter, comfortable with taking on responsibility, and capable of working solo from home. Appreciates a small-company environment where his/her contributions have an immediate impact
The ideal candidate will be available for face-to-face meetings with the Chief Operating Officer in downtown Toronto.

About Us
Panoramic Feedback is a specialized application service provider with an excellent international reputation. Our website: http://www.panoramicfeedback.com
Our system has provided hundreds of thousands of leaders with a broad perspective on their skills through the eyes of their colleagues, which supports them in developing their abilities to the full. Our system is used in over 50 languages (European, Cyrillic, Chinese, Japanese, Korean).
Panoramic Feedback was the first Internet-based 360-degree feedback system worldwide. 360 has gained acceptance as a major contributor to high standards of leadership.

Qualifications

  • CS degree or equivalent work experience. Minimum 3 years work experience
  • Superior skills and experience in Python
  • Working knowledge of SQL and database design
  • Familiarity with Linux web servers/command line
  • Experience developing web applications with an MVC framework, e.g. Django, Rails, TurboGears
  • A good understanding of how the Internet works, eg HTTP, REST
  • Experience with Ajax web applications
  • Familiarity with software development best-practices, including code review, automated testing, version control, and issue tracking
  • Keen awareness of emerging technologies and trends, best practices and design patterns

    The Job
    We have created a powerful user interface based on Python, PostgreSQL, TurboGears, ExtJS, Kid, Genshi, and C++, on a Linux platform.
    We rely heavily on a culture of testing, using unit tests, continuous integration, and Selenium functional testing.
    The challenge of this position is to end our dependence on the legacy C++ code base, so we can focus on developing new and interesting features.

    Developer's major responsibilities
  • Solo on-staff developer
  • Co-ordinate contract developers' work on occasion
  • Design architecture
  • Develop new code in Python
  • Provide last-tier tech support

    Salary
    Commensurate with experience.

    IMPORTANT: To differentiate your reply from spammers, please place these words "I AM REAL" at beginning of your reply. All others will be discarded
    Respond with a resume (preferably PDF) and a brief description of why you see yourself as a good fit for this role to: techjobs@panoramicfeedback dot com.
