Recent events

  • Tue Apr 05, Sy Ali went to the 2005-04 meeting. On the way back he chatted with Drew about adding a wiki. Although he resisted being given root on the spot to do it himself, it only took a couple of weeks before the three of Sy, Bill Thanis and Drew Sullivan got the wiki up on Saturday April the 23rd, 2005. Within days it entirely overtook the main website.
  • Tue May 03 (Executive meetings:2005-05) the execs made a judgement call to finally rename TLUG to GTALUG, although issues of what to do with the mailing list rename. This meeting was hosted by the budding LinuxCafe.
