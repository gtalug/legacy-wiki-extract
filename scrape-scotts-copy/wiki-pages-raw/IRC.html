GTALUG on Internet Relay Chat.

We can be found on the irc.freenode.net network of irc servers:

  • #gtalug

You can join us for live discussion on IRC by pointing your favourite IRC client to irc.freenode.net. A full list of servers is available at http://www.freenode.net/irc_servers.shtml.

Links to IRC clients can be found in the Linux IRC mini-HOWTO at The Linux Documentation Project.

The #toronto-lug IRC channel can sometimes be a little quiet, but it's easy to hang out there all day without keeping a full IRC client open. Just use Gaim. See IRC with Gaim for instructions.
