Mailing List Managers

There is a desire for GTALUG to migrate from Majordomo, which is looking pretty creaky, to something newer and less creaky.

From what I have heard from Drew's comments, the "problem" here is that managing the release of messages is a tougher problem than it might seem to be, and the management capabilities in Mailman aren't super-friendly, and don't scale particularly well as the number of admins increases.
