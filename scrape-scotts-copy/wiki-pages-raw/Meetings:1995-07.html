Dear Linuxers,

The next meeting of the Toronto Linux Users Group will be held
at 7 pm in the North York Public Library on Wednesday, July 26, 1995.

The featured speaker of the meeting will be Ken Ono, Head of Research
and Development at Angoss Software International, who will introduce
the Linux port of "Smartware".

"SmartWare" is a multi-platform  business application suite
which includes a wordprocessor, spreadsheet, and database, as well as other
productivity applications in a single integrated package.

A demo version of Smartware for Linux can be downloaded from Angoss' ftp site
at ftp.angoss.com:/pub/smartware/Demos.

The presentation will be followed by a question and answer session.

Location:   Meeting Room 2
            North York Public Library
            5120 Yonge Street

How to Get There:

           By the subway: take the Yonge subway
           to the "North York City Centre" stop.
           The library is at the southwest end of
           the mall connected to the subway.

           By automobile: The library is located at 5120
           Yonge Street, just a bit north of Sheppard Ave.
           Ample parking is available one block west of the
           library, on Beecroft Street. As well, there
           is underground parking accessible from
           Parkhome Ave.        

Everyone is welcome to attend. Be there!

  Regards,
