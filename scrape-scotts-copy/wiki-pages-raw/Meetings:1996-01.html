Dear Linuxers,

The next meeting of the Toronto Linux Users Group will be held
at 7:30 pm at the Swan Building, 5415 Dundas Street West,
on Tuesday, January 30, 1995.

The topic of this meeting's presentation will be the Empress
relational database system and its recently released native Linux
port, with a special focus on its Internet applications.  

The meeting will provide a hands-on demo of the Empress HTML toolkit
which provides a WWW interface for accessing the database.

Free copies of "Personal Empress" for Linux will be available
at the meeting.

We wish to thank our sponsor, Internet Direct for donating their
state-of-the-art multimedia conference room for this and
subsequent Linux meetings.

Location:   Suite 301
            5415 Dundas Street West
            Etobicoke          

How to Get There:

           By the subway: take the Bloor subway
           to the Kipling station.  The Swan building is
           two blocks west of the Kipling station,
           next to the large "Canadian Tire" plaza.

           For those who are driving, ample and free parking
           is available in the parking lot just south
           of the Swan building.

Everyone is welcome to attend. Be there!

        Cheers,
              Laszlo Herczeg
