Meetings:2005-09

From GTALUG

Meetings > August 2005

TLUG meetings are held at the University of Toronto on the second Tuesday of each month at 7:30 pm.

Date

Tuesday August 9, 2005

Time

7:30 pm

Topic

General Election and Q&A

Speaker

Drew Sullivan

Description

We are electing 3 new board members. Come view GTALUG politics at its best (worst). While counting votes will hold a general question and answer session.

The new board members are:

  • William Park
  • Christopher Browne
  • Seneca Cunningham

Location 

   Room GB248 or GB244, Galbraith Building, University of Toronto
   Room has not been assigned as this time so check both.
   Toronto, Ontario M5S 3G8 
   University of Toronto

Map
