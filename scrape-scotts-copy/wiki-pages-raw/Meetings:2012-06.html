Tuesday 12th June, 2012 at 7:30 pm

[edit]

Topic

Linux on ARM and the current Hardware/Distro Ecosystems w/ Scott Sullivan

[edit]

Description

The ARM ecosystem is a vibrant and dynamic space with Linux on top of almost all of it. This talk will take an overview look at the current state of where hardware and high-profile Linux distributions are in their push towards the mainstream.
