NewTLUG > Meetings

NewTLUG meetings are held the fourth Tuesday of each month (except in December)

Our meetings alternate between the IBM head office at 3600 Steeles Ave. East and Seneca College at Seneca@York on the York University campus.

The format of most meetings will be "Howto" and/or demonstration with special emphasis on the needs of users new to Linux, especially users arriving from a Windows environment.

If you have a particular topic you would like presented or if you have a topic YOU would like to present, please contact Herb Richter. (Please be sure to put something like "NewTLUG" in the subject line to better pass through the spam filter)
