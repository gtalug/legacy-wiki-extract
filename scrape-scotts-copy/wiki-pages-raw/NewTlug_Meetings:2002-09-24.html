NewTlug Meetings:2002-09-24

From GTALUG

This month's NewTLUG meeting is dedicated to our friend Jan Carlson.

Location: Linux Labs - 169 Eastern Ave, Toronto

Date, Time: Tuesday September 24th. 7:00 to 10:00 pm


Topic: The planned topic for this month (Networking 101) will be postponed until a presenter(s) is available, perhaps in a month or two.

For this month we will have the kind of meeting that I know Jan liked best - a general Linux question and answer session. There will be Linux machines available for hands on use.

Over the years Jan has helped so many people, especially people new to Linux. Please feel free to say a few words about how Jan helped you or something you learned from Jan. And if you can please demonstrate for the rest of us, some of what you learned or the help you received.


Directions: Eastern Ave is near the foot of the Don Valley Parkway.

(If I remember correctly) if driving down the DVP take the Richmond Street exit and then quickly get into the left lane to make the (almost a "U") turn onto Eastern. Map:
