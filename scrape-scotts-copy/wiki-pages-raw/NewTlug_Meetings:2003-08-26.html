NewTlug Meetings:2003-08-26

From GTALUG


This month's NewTLUG meeting will be held Tues Aug 26th., at the IBM offices 3600 Steeles Ave E.

Thanks: to Peter for helping NewTLUG with room arrangements and looking after the badges.

Badges: email PWArmstrong to pre-register

Date: Tues Aug 26th. Time: 7-10pm

Presenter:

Andrew Howlett - Andrew is a member of the Bruce Grey Linux Users' Group (bglug.org), centered in Owen Sound.

Topic:

Linux Games - Andrew will demonstrate some games that can be played on a Linux "work"station and some educational games such as Tux Typing. His presentation will include topics like downloading, installing and running free and commercial games plus running games under windows emulation such as wine and win4lin.

see: http://www3.sympatico.ca/howlettfamily/linux/games.pdf

Location: IBM offices 3600 Steeles Ave East, north side of Steeles at Pharmacy/Esna Park (between Victoria Park and Warden)

Parking: Parking is available in the visitor parkade from 6:00pm to 11:00pm.
