About

As my experience with both Linux and Perl increased, I discovered that I could make Apache part of my daily Linux desktop work and hobby thing. I built a site[1] around the concepts, and called it a psema4.

I never really could articulate well what the semaphore connection meant. Generally speaking[2], it was how I discovered the libre in foss.[3]

It's a development environment that opens communication between all manner of professions, specialties, and interests.

Anyway, after reading this article by Sy, and tinkering with Mediawiki a bit, I've been playing with the idea of moving it here.
