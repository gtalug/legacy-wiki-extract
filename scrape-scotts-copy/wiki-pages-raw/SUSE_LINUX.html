SUSE user comments

* Leah : I have used this distribution primarily since about 1998 and off an on before that. I worked for the US office of SUSE in the 1999-2000 time-frame doing random support work and assisting with the PPC distribution that was being developed at the time. I love the distribution and am happy to try to help anyone if I can, but don't be offended if it takes a while to get a response.
