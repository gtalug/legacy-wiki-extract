Linux specific:

POG GTALug Political Outreach Committee
Dealing with political issues that have an impact on the Linux community.
MUMU - Mississauga Ubuntu MeetUp
A group of Ubuntu users that meet once a month in a pub in Mississauga to share a beer, exchange information and talk just about anything.
Group site: http://tech.groups.yahoo.com/group/mumu-ca/
Wiki: https://wiki.ubuntu.com/mlug


WestTLUG - West Toronto Linux User Group
Meetings used to be every first Tuesday of the month.
WestTLUG Meeting
Alas, seems to be moribund...

Other GTA groups of interest:

CrisisCommonsTO
CrisisCommons is an online community that uses technology, translations and information to help relief agencies and disaster affected communities.
CrisisCamp volunteers work online and in city-based CrisisCamps, producing Tools and effort for disaster-affected communities and relief-related organizations.
Main Site
Drupal Toronto
For fans of the Drupal Wiki
Main Site
Django Toronto
This is a group for meeting other Toronto-based Django developers and other people who love Django and Python.
Mailing List
Free Geek Toronto - FGT
They divert hardware from landfill, refurbish it, install Ubuntu, then sell at affordable prices. Volunteers are rewarded with refurbished hardware. They also offer free computer hardware courses.
Free Geek Toronto
Greater Toronto Area BSD Users Group
The user group for Toronto BSD Unix fans. They normally meet on the third Wednesday of each month check website for details.
Main Site
Greater Toronto System Administrators Guild -- GTA SAGE
Main Site
The Hand Eye Society
A not-for-profit coalition of projects and people in support of Toronto's video game communities. Bi-monthly socials.
Main Site
The Internet Society, Canada Chapter
A chapter of ISOC, which advocates on digital issues on behalf of all web users. Parent body of the IETF.
The Internet Society, Canada
Perl Mongers Toronto -- Talk about perl
Group usually meets on last Thursday of every month @ 2 Bloor Street West, 8th or 16th floor
Main site
Planet Geek Toronto -- Refurbish and Install Linux on old PCs
Fixed up machines are then given away to users of a local foodbank.
Meets Sunday afternoons for work sessions.
Main site
Python GTA -- All sorts of Python stuff, meet at the Linuxcaffe Q&A then typically off to an Ice Cream place.
Meets every third tuesday of the month. (see next meeting)
PyGTA Front Page
Wiki
Toronto 3D Printers User Group
Meets last Monday of every month
http://www.toronto3dprinters.com/
Toronto Android Users Group
Meets periodically providing Android development workshops
http://www.torontoandroid.org/
Toronto Area Security Klatch - TASK
Deals with IT security issues on ALL platforms, from the *BSDs to Linux to smart phones to Windows. Membership is free.
Meetings are the last Wednesday of each month at 6:00 PM, see website for location.
http://www.task.to/
Toronto Asterisk Users Group -- TAUG -- VOIP and PBX software
Group usually meets on various Wednesdays of the month at the North York Civic Centre, 5100 Yonge St. (short walk from the North York Centre subway station)
Toronto Asterisk Users Group
Toronto Free-Net -- TFN -- Toronto's Oldest ISP
Toronto's not-for-profit volunteer-driven Internet Service Provider. Contact them to get at-cost Internet service and to volunteer. TFN is one of the last ISPs in Toronto that still offers dial-up service Dial-up. They are a Linux shop.
Toronto Free-Net
Toronto Lisp Users Group
Usually meets on the first Tuesday of the month, at LinuxCaffe.
Main Site
Toronto OpenStreetMap Enthusiasts
Irregular meetings.
Main Site
Toronto Ruby User Group -- TRUG
Meets are second Sunday of the month.
Main Site
Toronto Wireless Group -- TorWUG
Main Site
Unix Unanimous -- UU -- For Unix and all the Unix like operating systems
Meetings on every 2nd Wednesday of the month
Main Site
VMUG Toronto -- For VMware virtual machine users
Meets once per quarter.
All upcoming Canadian VMUG meetings
Wireless Toronto
Over 35 free to use volunteer supported wireless hotspots scattered around the GTA
