Yet Another Perl Conference, North America, 2005 Registration now open.

Conference dates
Monday - Wednesday 27 - 29 June 2005
Location
89 Chestnut Street
University of Toronto
Toronto, Ontario, Canada
Info at
http://yapc.org/America
http://yapc.org/America/register-2005.shtml
http://wiki.yapctoronto.org/

Direct registration

Full registration fee $85 (USD)

Book now for great deals on accommodations and ensure a space for yourself. See http://yapc.org/America/accommodations-2005.shtml


The schedule is up: http://www.yapc.org/America/schedule-2005/day1.html

From here, click on the "Day 2" and "Day 3" spots near the top to go from page to page. Click on a talk name to get details regarding the talk.

Speakers include Larry Wall, Allison Randal, Autrijus Tang, Brian Ingerson, Andy Lester, chromatic, brian d foy, Chip Salzenberg & Dan Sugalski... and many more!


It's shaping up to be a great conference!


Details of this announcement: http://yapc.org/America/registration-announcement-2005.txt
